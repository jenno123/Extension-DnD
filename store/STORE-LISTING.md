## Name
D&D Voice Overlay for Roll20

## Short description (max 132 chars)
Character portraits light up visual-novel style over Roll20 when a player talks in Discord voice. No webcams, no player setup.

## Detailed description
Make voice-only tabletop RPG sessions feel like a story-driven video game.

When a player speaks in your Discord voice channel, their character portrait
rises and lights up — visual-novel / JRPG dialogue style — right on top of your
Roll20 game. Idle characters stay hidden; the active speaker appears large with
their character name. No webcams, no video, no transcription.

Features:
• Speaking portraits that pop in from the side as people talk
• Large, transparent character art with name labels
• Click-through overlay — never blocks Roll20
• Pause your own overlay anytime (Ctrl+Shift+P) for out-of-character talk
• Works for the whole table; players just install and play

How it works: your game's host runs a small companion app that reads Discord
speaking events and sends them to a relay. This extension connects to that relay
and shows the portraits. Ask your host for the relay address (or it may be
pre-configured for your group).

Not affiliated with Discord, Roll20, or Wizards of the Coast.

## Privacy practices justification (for the dashboard)
- storage: remembers the relay URL on the user's device.
- tabs: lets the pause shortcut reach the Roll20 tab.
- host permission (app.roll20.net): the overlay is drawn only on Roll20 pages.
- Single purpose: display a speaking-character overlay on Roll20.
- No data is collected or transmitted to the developer.
